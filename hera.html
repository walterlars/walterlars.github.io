Problem

Outdated UI: A Major Hurdle

The Core Issue

The primary issue with the previous IMOD system was its outdated and unattractive user interface for their Desktop Version. The design was not only visually unappealing but also hindered efficiency and user experience. This outdated UI made it challenging for users to navigate the system effectively, leading to frustration and decreased productivity. A modern, intuitive redesign was necessary to enhance usability and overall satisfaction.

Solution

More Modern and Clean!

The new UI for the IMOD system has been meticulously designed to address these issues. It features a sleek, modern aesthetic that is both visually appealing and highly functional. Improved navigation and user-friendly interfaces make managing tasks more efficient, reducing frustration and increasing productivity. This updated design ensures that users can seamlessly integrate the IMOD system into their daily routines, balancing various commitments with ease.

The Design Process

Part 1

Initial Meeting and Understanding

The process began with a meeting with the CEO to understand the system's functionality and key requirements. I received a Demo Module to connect to my PC for hands-on testing.

Part 2

Research and Concept Development

The first approach was to redesign the aesthetics while maintaining the existing layout. I conducted research on various components, exploring how they could look and what colors would match the system. I Found inspiration for the Taskbar in Notion or Google Chrome how the Icons are presented and how the hover effect works. The Sliders and Buttons i got inspired in a variety of other software or on websites like Pinterest and Dribbble.

Part 3

Creating New Components

Using Figma, I designed new buttons, sliders, input fields, and other necessary components. This phase focused on ensuring the new design elements were both functional and visually appealing. I also tried to apply the 4px Rule and to make the whole Concept looking the same and to have similar or the same paddings.

Part 4

Implementation and Feedback

I integrated the new components with the Demo Module and sent the updated file to the company. Based on their feedback, I iterated on the design to make necessary changes. This collaborative effort ensured the design met all user needs.

Part 5

Final Touches

Upon request, I added a dark mode to the system, enhancing the overall user experience and providing a modern touch to the interface.
