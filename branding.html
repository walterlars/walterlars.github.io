Introduction

For this project, I explored my passion for packaging design through a series of fun and innovative creations. This personal endeavor allowed me to experiment with different design elements and styles, resulting in unique packaging solutions for chocolate, cans, and candles. Each design showcases my ability to blend functionality with aesthetics, creating visually appealing and user-friendly products. Through this project, I aimed to push the boundaries of conventional packaging design, making each item not just a product, but a memorable experience. It all was done by myself including the illustrations. I used the Adobe Cloud + Figma.
