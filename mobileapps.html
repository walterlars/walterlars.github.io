Software 1

DesAIgn

This prototype was created as a result of the UX Design 3 course at the university, where we, as a group of seven, conducted interviews with UX agencies on the topic of AI in the design industry. We analyzed these interviews and continuously processed the information using various techniques until we developed an idea for a supportive product. Our result was DesAIgn, an AI that assists in the design process and stores data from existing clients to facilitate new prototyping.

App 4

Spoody

This prototype was created as a result of the UX Design 1 course at the university, where we, as a group of four, were tasked with developing and visualizing a concept. The result was th app Spoody is an app to organize your days around fitness and healthy eating by allowing you to plan your week with meals and exercise sessions, create shopping lists, find recipes, and design and choose workouts. You also have a feature to see your progress and statistics as well as a feature to see the success of your friends.
